Team 3 Map Visualization

This visualization displays pollution concentration values gathered by multiple monitoring stations around the globe.

  • Globe drawn using an open source GeoJSON file.
  • Pollution Dataset provided by the WHO.
  • World City latitude and longitude values are an open source dataset.

Some challenges we ran into were finding a dataset for the city lat and lon coordinates for all our data points. Many are only commercially available for small towns that we had data from. We currently are able to draw ~70% of our dataset in their exact location. The other ~30% are accumulated and placed at the centroid of the country of origin. In a more perfect world, we would have latitude and longitude for every town no matter the size.
